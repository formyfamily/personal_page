About me

I’m Zhengfei Kuang (匡正非), a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Hao Li. Before USC, I was studying at Tsinghua University. I also worked as a research assistant in the Graphics & Geometric Computing Group from 2017 to 2019, advised by Prof. Shimin Hu.

Updates (06/2022): I graduated at USC as master and will transfer to Stanford as a new Ph.D. student this Fall. A brand new Ph.D. career! :P
